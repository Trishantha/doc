About Us

"Quality and convenience with economy sets us apart from our competitors"

Africa is already on an upward curve with foreign direct investment, NGO participation and organic development within the emerging countries, however the quality of the commercial office space is lagging way behind. EBC has introduced serviced office space starting in Malawi, that would be comparable in quality and service to any central business district in the world.

EBC plans to be at the forefront of the growth that is coming to Africa and for those businesses that are already here, together with those that are entering the market, EBC is set to provide a best in class solution to their office requirements.
